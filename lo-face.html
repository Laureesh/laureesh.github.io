üìù How to Use Each Product

1 CeraVe Foaming Facial Cleanser

When: Morning every day; Night on non-PanOxyl nights (use PanOxyl instead on Tue/Thu/Sat).

How to use:

  • Wet your face with lukewarm water
  • Dispense a nickel-sized amount into your palm
  • Gently massage over face and neck for 30‚Äì45 seconds (no need to scrub)
  • Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water until no suds remain
  • Pat dry with a clean towel
  • Follow with SA Lotion (morning) or Vitamin C serum ‚Üí PM moisturizer (night)
2 La Roche-Posay Toleriane Purifying Foaming Facial Cleanser

When: Morning & Night (main cleanser)

How to use:

  • Wet face with lukewarm water
  • Pump a small amount (quarter-sized) into your palm
  • Massage gently over your entire face and neck for 30-45 seconds
  • Pay extra attention to T-zone and jaw areas
  • Rinse thoroughly with water until no suds remain
  • Pat dry with a clean towel
3 PanOxyl Acne Foaming Wash (10% Benzoyl Peroxide)

When: Night routine on Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday (or as needed for breakouts)

How to use:

  • Wet face with lukewarm water
  • Pump a small amount (pea-sized) onto your fingertips
  • Gently massage over affected areas and entire face
  • Leave on for 1-3 minutes to let the benzoyl peroxide work
  • Rinse thoroughly with water
  • Pat dry gently
  • Note: May cause dryness or irritation ‚Äì use moisturizer immediately after
4 Paula's Choice 2% BHA Liquid Exfoliant

When: Morning routine, 2‚Äì3√ó weekly (on exfoliation days)

How to use:

  • After cleansing, make sure your face is completely dry
  • Pour 2-3 drops into your palm or apply directly to face
  • Gently spread across entire face, avoiding eye area and nose corners
  • Let it absorb for 5-10 minutes (will feel slightly sticky)
  • Follow with your regular moisturizer and SPF
  • Frequency: Start with 2√ó weekly, can increase to 3√ó if skin tolerates well
5 CeraVe SA Lotion (Salicylic Acid)

When: Morning routine (optional, after cleansing)

How to use:

  • Apply to damp or dry skin after cleansing
  • Use a small amount (quarter-sized) and spread evenly over face
  • Focus on areas prone to buildup or congestion
  • Wait 5 minutes before applying other products
  • Follow with moisturizer and SPF
  • Option: Use on body (shoulders, back, chest) instead of face if too strong
6 CeraVe AM Facial Moisturizing Lotion with SPF 30

When: Morning routine (final step)

How to use:

  • Apply to clean, dry skin after all serums and exfoliants
  • Use a nickel-sized amount for entire face
  • Gently massage into skin until absorbed
  • Allow 2-3 minutes before sun exposure for SPF to set
  • SPF benefit: Provides broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection
  • Reapply if you'll be outdoors for extended periods
7 La Roche-Posay Toleriane Double Repair UV SPF 30

When: Morning routine (SPF alternative)

How to use:

  • Apply after cleansing and serums
  • Use a pump-full for entire face and neck
  • Massage gently until fully absorbed
  • Wait 5 minutes before makeup or sun exposure
  • Can be layered under makeup
  • Reapply every 2 hours if exposed to direct sun
8 CeraVe Vitamin C Serum

When: Night routine (step 2)

How to use:

  • Apply to clean, dry skin after cleansing
  • Dispense 2-3 drops into your palm
  • Gently press onto face and neck
  • Allow 1-2 minutes to absorb
  • Follow with night moisturizer
  • Benefits: Brightens skin, reduces hyperpigmentation, antioxidant protection
9 CeraVe PM Facial Moisturizing Lotion

When: Night routine (final step)

How to use:

  • Apply after all serums have absorbed (vitamin C serum)
  • Use a nickel to quarter-sized amount
  • Massage gently into face and neck
  • Allow to fully absorb before bed (2-3 minutes)
  • Benefits: Overnight repair, ceramides restore moisture barrier
  • Can apply extra amount to dry patches if needed
